Like MAP-10000, this map of Middlesbrough, England, produced around 1942, provides a detailed view of the town's layout during that period. This map is from a different German military source, the Generalstab des Heeres (Military High Command). The map is of much larger scale, showing more detail of the town's streets, buildings, and infrastructure. Towards the bottom of the map, you can see Ayresome Park, the home stadium of Middlesbrough FC at the time.
