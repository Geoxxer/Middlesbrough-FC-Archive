Statue of George Hardwick at Riverside Stadium, Middlesbrough, England. Bronze outdoor sculpture of George Hardwick — former Middlesbrough FC defender and England international — mounted on a plinth outside the Riverside Stadium concourse. Photograph taken from ground level showing the full statue and part of its immediate surroundings.
