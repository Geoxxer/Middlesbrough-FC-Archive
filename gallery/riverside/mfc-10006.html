River Tees entrance to Middlehaven Dock, Middlesbrough, England. Exterior photograph showing the River Tees waterway where it enters Middlehaven Dock, with a vessel moored on the left and Riverside Stadium visible on the right. The image was taken from the riverbank looking towards the north eastern stands at ground level.
