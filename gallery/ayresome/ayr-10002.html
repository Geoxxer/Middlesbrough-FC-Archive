Ayresome Park, former home stadium of Middlesbrough Football Club, Middlesbrough, England. Photographed from an aerial perspective, this image captures Ayresome Park surrounded by a vast urban landscape of Middlesbrough and it's housing in 1980, showcasing the stadium's architecture and its integration within the town.
