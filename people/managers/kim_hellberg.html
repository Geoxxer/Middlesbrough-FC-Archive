Kim Hellberg

Kim Hellberg just came back from a successful period managing Hammarby IF in Sweden, where he led the team to consecutive 2nd place finishes in the Allsvenskan in 2024 and 2025. Hellberg signed a two-and-a-half-year contract with Middlesbrough FC on the 24th November 2025, taking over from interim manager Adi Viveash, who had been in charge since the departure of Rob Edwards earlier that month.
